Ahmad Taghinezhad ,Ph.D.

Senier Lecturer of Computer Enginnering at University of Tabriz

Ahmad Taghinezhad-Niar presently holds the position of Adjunct Professor within the domain of computer engineering at University of Tabriz, located in Tabriz, Iran. He stands as an academic figure, having successfully attained his Ph.D. and Master's degrees in computer engineering from the aforementioned institution, during the years 2021 and 2017, respectively.
Dr. Taghinezhad's academic endeavors have extended beyond the borders of his home institution. His commitment to advancing knowledge is evidenced by his tenure as a Visiting Researcher in the realm of Big Data, supported by the Erasmus+ program, at the University of Bonn situated in Bonn, Germany, during the year 2018.
Notably, Dr. Taghinezhad has garnered distinction through his participation and accomplishments in diverse programming competitions, substantiating his prowess within the discipline. Moreover, he has been the beneficiary of various national elite foundation initiatives in Iran, thereby underscoring his significance within the academic landscape of the nation.
Dr. Taghinezhad is duly recognized as a member of the exceptional talents cohort at the University of Tabriz, a testament to his academic prowess and contributions. Beyond his instructional duties, he has extended his academic influence as a peer reviewer for esteemed scholarly journals of prominence. His critical insights have thus contributed to the rigorous evaluation and dissemination of scholarly work within his field.
Within the realm of research, Dr. Taghinezhad's interests encompass a spectrum of vital topics, including Scheduling Algorithms, Distributed Systems, Cloud Computing, Internet of Things (IoT), and Formal Methods. This diverse array of research pursuits mirrors his comprehensive engagement with pivotal themes within contemporary computer engineering and affirms his role as a dynamic scholar within the discipline.
